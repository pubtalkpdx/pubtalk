Pub Talk PDX

Meaningful conversations for men

About

Pub Talk is a social group that gathers men together to develop relationships that go deeper than news, sports, and weather.

When & Where

Last Thursday of each month (except June–August and December)

Golden Valley Brewing, Beaverton, Oregon

Drinks at 6:00 PM | Program begins at 6:30 PM

Meal and beverages provided. Donations welcome.

Format

  • Softball question to start conversation
  • Speaker shares a personal, impactful story
  • Hardball question to deepen discussion

Ground Rules

  • What is said at Pub Talk stays at Pub Talk
  • Don’t try to fix anyone
  • You can always pass
